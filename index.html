FAQs

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Please reach out to us.

What types of projects can Vertex handle?

We usually prefer to take project from scratch starting by UX research to UI design and we then integrate our developer by letting the client choose the platform they want to use either Webflow or custom web development.

Why we recommend using Webflow?

It is very easy for the client to use, have low cost of maintenance with zero plugin to worry about. With Webflow we can ensure that the visual quality of the website will be of high quality and very close to the design.

What is a timeframe for a website project?

It depends on the complexity of the project. Normally we deliver basic website from 3 to 4 weeks. For now, complex functionalities will vary from 6 to 8 weeks.

I already have the Figma design, I need a Webflow developer.

Absolutely, our designer will do an audit on your design to make sure it has all the required elements for the development to take on.
